## 1. What are lifecycle hooks? What are lifecycle hooks used for?
A window in to how our library is really working behing the scenes. You could expect to use a lifecycle hook whenever you need to watch a piece of data either at for instance page load or when leaving a page.
## 2. How have you utilized lifcycle hooks in your afternoon projects?
So far I have been utilizing onComputed Hook which is the watcher as a whole.
## 3. What are mounting hooks? When might you use them?
Mounting hooks occur before the page actually renders or before you were to leave a page. You might use this when you have data that always needs to persist on your page and needs to be loaded right off of the bat.
